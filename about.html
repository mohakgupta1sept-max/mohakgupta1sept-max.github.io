Why we publish these guides

Every week we see customers overpay for specs they don't need, or buy used machines with problems they couldn't have spotted — 4GB HDD laptops sold as "perfect for study", used machines with SSDs on their last legs, overpriced older i7s sold on the strength of the sticker alone.
