Why this site exists

RMG Tech Solutions · Delhi, India

I work at a hardware repair and resale shop in Wazirpur computer market, Delhi — one of the busiest used-computer markets in North India. My daily work is repairing laptops, testing used machines, building PCs, and helping customers pick the right hardware for their budget.

A large share of our customers are students preparing for SSC, banking, railways and other competitive exams. They come with a tight budget, a family counting on them, and almost no way to verify what a seller tells them. I've watched students get sold 4GB HDD laptops "perfect for study", used machines with SSDs on their last legs, and overpriced i7s from 2014 sold on the strength of the "i7" sticker alone.

RMG Tech Solutions is the advice I give across the counter, written down. No spec-sheet copying, no brand favoritism — just what actually matters when you use and repair these machines every day.

What you'll find here

  • Buying guides for student budgets (₹15,000–35,000 range mostly)
  • The exact physical and software checks we run on used laptops at the shop
  • Plain explanations of specs — what matters, what's marketing
